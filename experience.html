OUTREACH PROGRAM: IM 2 YOU WITH AL-NASUHA

19 MAY 2023

Large group projects with classmates. The event went to Gombak, Selangor. As is happening with many of us either before or after the program, these memories will never be forgotten.

6 JULY 2023

This is our last program as a class before moving on to the next level. We were extremely happy and overwhelmed to be able to assemble everyone for the lectures on this night, and many of them provided advice and words of support. These are the most expensive memories we've made this semester.
