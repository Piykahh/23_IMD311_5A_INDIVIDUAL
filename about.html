About me

Know More About Me!

Hi, my name is Wan Ili Afiqah Binti Wan Norazmi, and I am currently in the last semester of this semester. I have been at UiTM Machang, Kelantan, for 3 years; I started at the start of 2021 in the COVID-19 era and have thoroughly enjoyed myself here. The university is great; the people and vibe are fantastic, and the atmosphere is one that makes you actually want to go to university. Before I was a student at UiTM Machang, I studied at the Methodist Girl Secondary School from Form 1 until Form 5. I was born and raised in Lumut, Perak, and grew up in Kuantan.
